Charles Puelz

Welcome to my website! I am a postdoc/instructor at the Courant Institute, funded in part by an RTG grant (Aleks Donev, PI). Before I was postdoc working with Boyce Griffith in his Cardiovascular Modeling and Simulation Lab.

My research interests are in mathematical modeling, numerical methods for partial differential equations, cardiovascular mechanics, and fluid dynamics.

My twin brother is a postdoc in statistics at the Chicago Booth business school. His website can be found here.
